What We Do

Create your experience edge with Xenia

Guest Experience

Xenia is fully integrated, end-to-end hotel management software that seamlessly supports You in delivering the best Guest experience possible.

Tailored Service

Xenia is scalable, fully configurable and creates easily accessed visibility over your bookings, housekeeping, laundry, minibar, cashflow and inventory. So at any one time, you know precisely what’s needed to tailor the perfect guest experience.

Affordable

Best of all, the advanced functionality and enhanced management reporting wrapped up in Xenia is surprisingly affordable!

Benefits

Smart, switched-on hotel operators who know the value of increased every day efficiency and accurate reporting:

  • More self-generated bookings — less reliance on channel partners
  • Stronger direct relationships with your clients and guests
  • More efficient operations around check-in, check-out, group bookings, cancellations, room service and meals, tour and function management
  • Efficient staffing, rostering and internal communication
  • Better housekeeping and front desk management
  • Restocking and theft management
  • Better back—end financial management and cashflow visibility
  • Smooth, integrated operations
  • 24/7 on—call support

Empower Your Guests

Xenia takes some of the load off your shoulders by empowering guests to design and modify their own guest experience

Through the Xenia mobile app, your guests can

  • Manage self-check-in / check—out
  • Receive and send alerts for delays and emergencies
  • Manage booking preferences
  • Pre—book meals, room service and excursions
  • Avoid front desk queues
  • Track accounts as they stay
  • Have important information such as wi-fi passwords at their fingertips
  • Along with a map of the hotel
